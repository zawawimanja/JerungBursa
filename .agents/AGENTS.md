# Project-Scoped Rules

## Swing Trading Selection Rules (Bawah RM1.00)
When evaluating, scanning, or recommending stocks for swing trading in the JerungBursa dashboard, prioritize stocks that adhere strictly to these 3 criteria:

1. **Trend & SMA Alignment (Uptrend Priority):** 
   - The stock price MUST be trading above its 50-day moving average (SMA50) and 200-day moving average (SMA200) to confirm a healthy uptrend. 
   - Avoid recommending downtrending stocks that are trading below their SMA50 (even if they have many floor touches), as they carry high overhead selling pressure.

2. **Ultra-Tight Risk & Floor Solidity:** 
   - Prioritize stocks with a mature, solid support base (at least 4x floor touches, ideally 8x to 10x) showing active accumulation.
   - The distance between the current price and the support floor must be extremely tight (ideally <= 4%, and less than 2% for ultimate safety) so that Stop Loss orders can be set close to the floor (limiting maximum risk to <= 5%).

3. **Fresh IPOs & Confluence Breakouts (VCP Base + Volume Spike):**
   - Focus on fresh IPOs (listed within the last 2-3 years) because they lack historical overhead resistance.
   - Look for the double confluence of a tight VCP (Staircase) pattern combined with a recent volume/turnover spike (Turnover >= RM500k today) indicating institutional shark (Jerung) entry.
